## 2. Final Terminology Decisions

### The Green Print

The root/canonical document of BANANOW Land.

It explains the soil, seeds, Crops, FAMILY, Future Literacy Layer (Web3 & AI), BANANOW NFT layer, and public direction of BANANOW.

It is not:

- a legal document,
- a financial promise,
- a tokenomics paper,
- a marketplace proposal,
- an operating log,
- an implementation specification,
- or a guaranteed roadmap.

---

### Land

BANANOW’s world, field, portal, and shared growing space.

Land connects:

- digital culture,
- real-world activity,
- community,
- education,
- creative economy,
- parenting/family support,
- AI literacy,
- Web3/blockchain literacy,
- and BANANOW’s playful identity.

---

### FAMILY

The human community layer of BANANOW.

FAMILY includes people who visit, read, ask, learn, share, hold, build, support, care, and help the Land stay alive.

FAMILY is not a rigid hierarchy.

It is a set of participation signals.

---

### The Crops

BANANOW’s brands, programs, media, tools, services, and experiments.

A Crop can be:

- a media platform,
- an education program,
- a family support tool,
- a Web3 identity layer,
- a creative service,
- a real-world activity,
- or an experiment planted for a future season.

---

### Future Proof Crops

Forward-facing Crops prepared to grow through future changes.

Current Future Proof Crops:

- Na Now News
- Skool Now
- Claps
- BANANOW NFT / Heads
- future programs when the Land is ready

Future Proof Crops are directions of growth, not guaranteed finished products or financial promises.

---

### Earlier Crops

Earlier seeds planted in previous BANANOW seasons.

Earlier Crops are not dead, failed, abandoned, old, or deprecated by default.

They may be:

- idle,
- resting in the soil,
- remembered as roots,
- merged into new Crops,
- reactivated later,
- or used as historical/cultural references.

Earlier Crops include:

- 90’s Vibes
- Dermaga Web3
- Class Now
- InAMotion
- Agent Now
- NOTA’s Dept.

Final phrase:

> Earlier does not mean finished. Earlier means planted before.

---

### Sprouts

Newcomers, curious visitors, and early participants.

Sprouts may be discovering BANANOW, Web3, AI, the Crops, the FAMILY, or the Land itself.

Sprouts do not need to hold NFTs, understand blockchain, use AI tools, or perform a role immediately.

Curiosity is enough.

---

### Lovers

Active supporters and warm community members.

Lovers support the Land by reading, sharing, joining, inviting, attending, giving feedback, learning together, and keeping the vibe alive.

Lovers are not defined by NFT ownership.

They are defined by warmth and presence.

---

### Heads

BANANOW NFT holders and Web3 identity participants.

Heads carry a BANANOW signal on-chain.

Safe framing:

- collectible,
- community signal,
- cultural marker,
- on-chain memory,
- Web3 identity layer.

Heads are not promised:

- profit,
- revenue,
- marketplace liquidity,
- guaranteed utility,
- guaranteed future access,
- rewards,
- or benefits.

---

### Farmers

Caretakers/operators of BANANOW Land.

Farmers help:

- maintain the soil,
- tend the Crops,
- guide rhythm,
- protect tone,
- write,
- build,
- host,
- coordinate,
- document,
- support,
- and keep BANANOW healthy, fair, playful, and useful.

Farmers are not rulers.

They are caretakers.

---

### Future Literacy Layer (Web3 & AI)

BANANOW’s learning and practice layer for future-facing technology.

AI helps people:

- think,
- create,
- process,
- learn,
- assist,
- reflect,
- organize,
- and care.

Blockchain/Web3 helps people:

- verify,
- own,
- record,
- coordinate,
- preserve on-chain memory,
- signal digital identity,
- and decentralize participation.

AI is not forced to be a subset of Web3.

AI and Web3 are companion literacy layers inside BANANOW’s future-facing direction.

BANANOW plays, learns, and works across both.
